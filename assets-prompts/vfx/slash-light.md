# 轻斩特效 (Light Slash VFX)

## 基本信息
- **类型**: vfx
- **用途**: 轻攻击命中时的斩击特效
- **尺寸**: 64x64
- **动画帧数**: 4帧
- **输出格式**: PNG, sprite sheet（横向排列，64x256）

## 风格标准
> Dark fantasy pixel art, 16-bit retro style, gloomy medieval atmosphere, muted desaturated colors with warm accent lighting (amber/orange from fire), high contrast shadows, hand-crafted pixel detail.

```
Dark fantasy pixel art VFX sprite sheet, 16-bit retro style, light slash effect, 4 frames horizontal sprite sheet, 64x64 per frame, white and silver arc slash trail, dark souls style weapon trail, slash appearing then fading, transparent background, game VFX asset, pixel perfect, sword trail effect with sparks on impact, muted white to grey gradient
```

## 参考说明
- 风格参考: Dead Cells的斩击特效
- 动作: 出现→扩散→消散
- 关键帧: 出现(1帧)→扩散(1帧)→消散(2帧)

## 备注
- 特效应跟随武器轨迹
- 命中时应有额外的火花效果
- 颜色应与武器属性匹配（白色/银色）
